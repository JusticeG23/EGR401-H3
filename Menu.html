About Levels

  • The Obstacle Course level is an adventurous level where the
    player goes through different segments. This includes a wall
    jump climb, running on a narrow road containing 6 large swinging
    hammers, and running through a hill with falling objects.


  • The Harpoon level is a thrilling level where the 4 players stand
    in a boat and throws harpoons at fish in the water. The regular
    fish are positive points, where the red fish are negative
    points.
